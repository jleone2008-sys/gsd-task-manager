How it works

Up and running in under a minute.

Sign in, type your tasks, start checking things off. That's genuinely all there is to it.

01
Dump your brain
Sign in with Google or email in seconds. Then start typing everything on your mind — tasks, reminders, ideas. No formatting, no categories needed to start.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Honest answers to common questions.

Is GSD actually free?
+
Yes — no credit card, no trial, no freemium catch. GSD is free because we believe the best task manager is one you actually use.
What makes this different from Todoist or Notion?
+
Those are powerful tools built for people who enjoy building systems. GSD is built for people who just want to finish tasks. No views to configure, no templates to pick, no onboarding flow.
Do I need to create an account?
+
One click with Google and you're in, or sign up with email in about 20 seconds. Tasks sync across every device from the first moment.
Is my data private?
+
Completely. Your tasks are encrypted and never sold, used for ads, or analyzed. We don't know what's on your list, and that's exactly how it should be.
Can I delete my account?
+
Yes. Go to account settings and delete — your data is gone within 30 days. No guilt trip, no winback campaign.
Does it work on mobile?
+
Yes. The app is fully responsive and works great on any device. Add it to your home screen for a native app feel. iOS and Android apps are on the roadmap.
